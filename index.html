ABOUT US

My name is Tatyana Gordeeva. Together with my husband and daughter I live in Ukraine. I have been breeding Persian cats for over 10 years. Our pets are part of our friendly family. They receive all our love and care, which they need. Under "Available" you can see if we have kittens. If you are interested, please contact me at the contact information listed on the website. Thank you for your visit!

The Persian cat is one of the most beautiful of all domestic breeds. It is a true aristocrat, which in an incredible way combines an unparalleled appearance, intelligence and regal manners of behavior with an amazing affection and sincere love for its owner. Due to this harmonious combination Persian cat is confidently ahead in popularity rating of other breeds.
